Our Capability

What 4LCORP Brings

Not a concept. An operating multi-metal recovery company with international refining capacity, an academic R&D partner, and direct feedstock access.

⚙ 8 Metals in Production

Silver, tungsten, copper, cobalt, zinc, lead, iron, gold. 6 of 8 on the 2025 USGS Critical Minerals List.

🌎 Global Refining Operations

Active recovery capacity in Indonesia and Africa. Scaling what exists, not starting from zero.

🎓 Texas A&M University

Academic partnership: process optimization, impurity removal, next-gen recovery for low-concentration streams.

🔒 Hydrometallurgical Process

Selective separation for simultaneous multi-metal recovery. Reduced chemical use. Closed-loop water systems.

📦 Diversified Feedstock

Domestic e-waste collection networks plus international partner streams. Supply is not single-source.

🏆 Multi-Metal Advantage

Co-recovery of cobalt, copper, zinc, tungsten, silver simultaneously. Most competitors target one metal.

Technical Approach

Project Objectives & Timeline

60-month project. Construct and operate a commercial-scale U.S. facility recovering multiple critical minerals from electronic waste.

Innovation

Multi-Metal Co-Recovery

Simultaneous recovery of cobalt, copper, zinc, tungsten, silver in one integrated hydrometallurgical flow. Reduced chemical consumption. Closed-loop water. Texas A&M R&D on higher yields and impurity control for low-concentration streams.

Supply Chain Impact

Direct Import Reduction

Addresses cobalt 79%, copper 57%, zinc 73%, silver 77%, tungsten >50% import reliance. Feedstock from domestic e-waste plus Indonesia and Africa. Offtake to battery manufacturers, defense contractors, electronics firms.
